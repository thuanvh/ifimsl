Detailed Description

Representation of a 3D rotation as an angle-axis or axial vector.

Author:
BJ Tordoff
Date:
02/11/2002 Although useful for somethings, angleaxis vectors are the poor mans alternative to using genuine quaternions mainly because the mathematics of products is much less simple (and more computationally costly).
