Detailed Description

A 3D normalisation object.

Author:
BJ Tordoff
Date:
15/8/2003 This class is designed for normalising points and lines (segments) in 3D euclidean space, such as must be done before calculating the various 3D transforms and 3D to 2D projections. A variety of normalisations are possible, but the most common and simplest is to simply provide a single distance which is assumed to be the dimension of a cube containing all points.

X, Y and Z are scaled equally. Doing otherwise complicates things and is rarely needed.
