Fast approximation to arc-tangent over full range.

Author:
DW Murray
Date:
02/08/02 This version of atan2 calculates an approximation to atan(y/x) using a linear method. As x,y are passed seperately, their signs are used to determine the quadrant of the result, allowing full range recovery of the result.
