Detailed Description

Evaluation of real polynomials at real and complex points.

vnl_real_polynomial represents a univariate polynomial with real coefficients, stored as a vector of doubles. This allows evaluation of the polynomial at given values of , or of its derivative .

Roots may be extracted using the roots() method.
